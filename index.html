Project

Comic Trailer



What was on my mind?

With my trailer, my goal was to create something that invoked a lot questions and made the user feel what it feels like to be my character (Carl) for a day. To do this, I attempted to gamify the entire experience and let the user try to guess what the story was behind Carl and what he was capable of, as they navigate the trailer.

Things I wanted to improve

  • Adding music
  • Smoother transitions between scenes
  • Smoother smoother parallax effects
  • Better signs of life
  • Clearer graphics without pixelation

Project

Motion Comic book



What was on my mind?

For my comic, I wanted to transport the user into the graphical and interesting world of Carl - my main character. Mu comic is meant to be an immersive and interesting experience, blending together different elements to create a digital comic that brought life to Carl and his world, and made his world exciting to understand and delve into as a user.

Things I wanted to improve

  • Better animations of typography
  • Better animations of characters
  • Better animations of environment
  • Specific sounds for certain frames
  • Better signs of life
  • Clearer graphics without pixelation
